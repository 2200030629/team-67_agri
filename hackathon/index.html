feed crops

feed crop products,such as oats and alfalfa,are harvested for livestock consumption

fiber crops

fiber crop products,such as cotton and hemp,are harvested for textile and paper products.Most fibers used to make textiles are taken from the stem or roots of plants such as flax

oil crops

oil crop products,such as canola and corn,are harvested for consumption or industrial uses

ornamental crops

ornamental crop products,such as dogwood and azalea,are harvested for landscape gardening

industrial crops

industrial crop products,such as rubber and tobacco,are harvested for their products use in factories or machies
